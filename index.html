Frequently Asked Questions

Everything you need to know about our services

What video formats do you support?

+

We support all major video formats including MP4, MOV, AVI, and ProRes. We can also work with raw footage from most professional cameras.

How does your messaging app workflow work?

+

Once you assign a project, you'll get access to our dedicated messaging app where you can communicate directly with your assigned editor, track progress in real-time, provide feedback, and receive updates 24/7.

Do you provide revisions?

+

Yes! We include 2 rounds of revisions in our standard packages. Additional revisions can be added for an extra fee.

Can you work with my existing footage?

+

Absolutely! We can work with any footage you provide. We'll assess the quality and recommend the best approach for your project.
